Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the bank's chatbot work?

The chatbot is designed to assist you with a variety of banking queries, including account management, loan information, and more. Simply type your question, and the bot will provide instant answers or guide you through specific banking services.

Can I speak to a human agent?

Yes! If you prefer to speak with a human representative, the chatbot can escalate your query to a live agent who will assist you further.

What information do I need to use the chatbot?

You may need to provide your account number or other details, depending on the nature of your inquiry. The chatbot will prompt you for the necessary information to assist you effectively.

Is my data secure with the chatbot?

Absolutely! We take privacy and security seriously. All communications with the chatbot are encrypted, and your personal information is never shared without your consent.
